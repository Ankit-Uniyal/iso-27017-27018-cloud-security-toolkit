# Cloud ISMS Internal Audit Programme (ISO 27017 / 27018)

**Document ID:** CLOUD-IAP-001 | **Version:** 1.0 | **Classification:** Internal | **Owner:** Internal Audit Lead | **Review:** Annual

## 1. Purpose

This Programme defines the planning, conduct and reporting of internal audits of the Cloud ISMS in compliance with ISO/IEC 27001:2022 clause 9.2 (Internal Audit) and the cloud-specific extensions of ISO/IEC 27017:2015 and ISO/IEC 27018:2019.

## 2. Audit Objectives

- Verify the Cloud ISMS conforms to ISO/IEC 27001/27017/27018 and the organisation's own requirements
- Verify the Cloud ISMS is effectively implemented and maintained
- Identify nonconformities, risks and improvement opportunities
- Provide objective assurance to top management

## 3. Audit Scope (3-year rolling cycle)

| Year | Focus Areas | Standards |
|------|-------------|-----------|
| Year 1 | All clauses 4-10 + CLD.6.3.1, CLD.8.1.5, CLD.9.5.1/2, A.1-A.5 | 27001/27017/27018 |
| Year 2 | All clauses + CLD.12.1.5, CLD.12.4.5, CLD.13.1.4, A.6-A.10 | 27001/27017/27018 |
| Year 3 | Full SoA verification + A.11; vendor/sub-processor sample | 27001/27017/27018 |

## 6. Audit Process

1. **Preparation** — review previous audit reports, NCRs, risk register, SoA.
2. **Opening meeting** — confirm scope, schedule, resources.
3. **Fieldwork** — sample evidence, interview personnel, observe operations.
4. **Daily wrap-ups** — communicate emerging issues.
5. **Closing meeting** — present findings.
6. **Reporting** — issue audit report within 10 working days.
7. **Follow-up** — track corrective actions to closure.

## 7. Sampling Approach

| Area | Sampling Basis | Min Sample |
|------|----------------|-----------|
| Access reviews | Per business unit | 3 BUs |
| Cloud configurations | Per CSP account | 5 accounts |
| Sub-processor reviews | Per sub-processor | All high-risk |
| DSR / breach handling | Per quarter | 5 each |
| Change records | Per quarter | 10 changes |
