| Role | Required Competencies | Evidence |

| Incident Responder | GCIH/GCFA or equivalent + CSP IR | Certificates |
| Internal Auditor | ISO 27001 Lead Auditor | Certificate |
| All staff | Annual security & privacy awareness | LMS log |

## PART B — Communications Plan

### B.1 Internal Communications

| Audience | Topic | Channel | Frequency | Owner |
|----------|-------|---------|-----------|-------|
| Top management | KPI dashboard, risk highlights | Mgmt review | Quarterly | CISO |
| Engineering | Cloud security guidance, paved-road updates | Internal docs, Slack | Continuous | CSO |
| All staff | Awareness, policy reminders | Email, LMS | Quarterly | CISO |
| GRC + Auditors | Audit programme, NCRs | Audit calendar, repo | Per cycle | Audit Lead |
| HR + Legal | Privacy obligations, training | Joint working group | Quarterly | DPO |
| Sales / CSM | Customer security positioning | Trust portal, FAQs | Continuous | Trust Lead |

### B.2 External Communications

| Audience | Topic | Channel | Trigger | Owner |
|----------|-------|---------|---------|-------|
| Customers | Security-posture updates | Trust Centre / portal | Quarterly + on event | CISO/Trust |
| Customers | Sub-processor changes | Email + portal | 30-day notice | DPO |
| Customers | Security incidents (PII) | Email per DPA | Within SLA | DPO/CISO |
| Regulators | Notifiable breach | Formal letter | Within statutory period | Legal/DPO |
| Public | Privacy notice | Website | On change | DPO |
| CSPs | Sub-processor / risk topics | Account team | As needed | CSO |

### B.3 Crisis Communications

- Pre-approved breach notification templates (per jurisdiction) maintained by Legal+DPO.
- Spokesperson: CISO for security; DPO for privacy; CEO for reputational.
- Holding statement publishable within 1 hour of confirmed major incident.

### B.4 Records

- Comms log retained 7 years.
- All breach communications logged in NCR / Incident register.
