# Cloud ISMS — Interested Parties Register

**Document ID:** CLOUD-IPR-001 | **Version:** 1.0 | **Classification:** Internal | **Owner:** CISO | **Review:** Annual

## 1. Purpose

Satisfies ISO/IEC 27001:2022 clause 4.2 — identifies interested parties relevant to the Cloud ISMS and their requirements / expectations.

## 2. Register

| ID | Interested Party | Type | Needs / Expectations | Compliance Obligations | Communication Channel | Owner |
|----|------------------|------|----------------------|------------------------|-----------------------|-------|
| IP-01 | Customers (B2B) | External | Confidentiality, availability, 27017/27018 evidence | Contractual SLA, DPA | Account Management, security portal | CRO |
| IP-02 | Data subjects (PII principals) | External | Privacy, DSR fulfilment | GDPR, UAE PDPL, India DPDPA | Privacy notice, DSR portal | DPO |
| IP-03 | Regulators | External | Demonstrable compliance | All applicable laws | Formal correspondence | Legal |
| IP-04 | Cloud Service Providers (AWS, Azure, GCP) | External | Adherence to acceptable use | CSP T&Cs | Account team, support | CTO |
| IP-05 | Sub-processors | External | DPA terms, security minimums | Contract obligations | Vendor management | Procurement |
| IP-06 | Insurers | External | Demonstrated control maturity | Cyber-insurance terms | Annual renewal | CFO |
| IP-07 | External auditors / certification body | External | Audit access, evidence | ISO certification scheme | Audit calendar | Compliance |
| IP-08 | Investors / Board | Internal | Risk transparency | Governance | Board pack | CEO |
| IP-09 | Top management | Internal | Strategic alignment, no surprises | ISMS oversight | Management Review | CISO |
| IP-10 | Employees / contractors | Internal | Clear policies, training | Acceptable use | Awareness programme | HR |
| IP-11 | Information-security team | Internal | Resources, mandate | Internal SLAs | Tickets, ChatOps | CISO |
| IP-12 | Engineering / Product | Internal | Paved-road controls, low friction | Internal policies | Architecture review | CTO |
| IP-13 | DPO / Privacy team | Internal | Privacy-by-design, DSR support | Privacy laws | Privacy council | DPO |
| IP-14 | Industry bodies (CSA, ISACA) | External | Information sharing | None compulsory | Membership | Compliance |
| IP-15 | Local communities (large-region datacentres) | External | Sustainability, transparency | Voluntary | Annual report | ESG |

## 3. Requirements Mapped to ISMS Outputs

| Requirement | ISMS Element |
|-------------|--------------|
| 27017/27018 audit evidence (IP-01, IP-07) | SoA + worked examples |
| DSR within statutory deadline (IP-02) | DSR procedure |
| Region pinning (IP-01, IP-03) | Cross-Border Transfer Procedure |
| Sub-processor transparency (IP-01, IP-05) | Sub-processor list |
| Breach notification SLA (IP-01, IP-03) | Breach Response Procedure |
| Board reporting (IP-08, IP-09) | KPI Dashboard, Management Review |

## 4. Review Triggers

- New customer segment / region
- New regulator engagement
- Significant change in CSP or sub-processor portfolio
- Annual review
