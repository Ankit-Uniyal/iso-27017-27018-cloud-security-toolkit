# 10 — Improvement (ISO 27001 Clause 10)

## Purpose
This folder consolidates artefacts that demonstrate how the Cloud ISMS is continually improved in line with ISO/IEC 27001:2022 Clause 10, ISO/IEC 27017:2015 and ISO/IEC 27018:2019. It captures non-conformities, corrective actions, lessons learned and improvement initiatives arising from audits, incidents, management reviews and supplier reviews.

## Contents
- `CLOUD-NCS-CAR-LOG.md` — Non-conformity & Corrective Action register (open / closed items, root cause, ECD, verification of effectiveness).
- `CONTINUAL-IMPROVEMENT-LOG.md` — Forward-looking improvement initiatives with owner, target date, KPI impact and status.

## How to use
1. Raise a record whenever an audit, incident, supplier review, control test or management review identifies a weakness, deviation or improvement opportunity.
2. Classify the entry as a Non-Conformity (NC), Observation (OBS) or Opportunity for Improvement (OFI).
3. Perform root-cause analysis (5-Whys, fishbone or equivalent) for every NC.
4. Record correction (immediate fix) and corrective action (systemic fix) separately.
5. Track to closure and verify effectiveness after at least one operating cycle before closing.
6. Feed aggregated trends into the next Management Review (folder 09).

## Linked clauses & controls
- ISO/IEC 27001:2022 Clauses 9.2, 9.3, 10.1, 10.2
- ISO/IEC 27002:2022 controls 5.1, 5.35, 5.36, 6.8, 5.27 (lessons learned)
- ISO/IEC 27017:2015 cloud-specific guidance for shared-responsibility incidents
- ISO/IEC 27018:2019 PII breach handling and remediation
