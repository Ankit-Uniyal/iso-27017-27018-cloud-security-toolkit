## PART A — Cloud Service Privacy Notice (Customer-Facing)

### 1. Who We Are

[Provider Legal Name] ("we", "us") provides cloud-based services to our business customers. This notice describes how we handle Personal Data when we act as a controller. When we act as a processor on behalf of a customer, that customer's privacy notice applies; we process Personal Data only on their documented instructions.

### 2. Personal Data We Collect

| Category | Examples | Source |
|----------|----------|--------|
| Account & contact | Name, business email, phone | Provided by you |
| Authentication | Username, MFA factors, login times, IP | Service usage |
| Billing | Invoice address, payment method ID | Provided by you / payment processor |
| Service usage | Feature usage, performance telemetry | Automatic |
| Support | Communications with us | You / our staff |

### 3. Purposes & Lawful Bases (GDPR / equivalents)

| Purpose | Lawful Basis |
|---------|--------------|
| Provide the Service | Contract |
| Customer support | Contract / Legitimate interest |
| Billing & finance | Contract / Legal obligation |
| Service improvement (aggregated/pseudonymised) | Legitimate interest |
| Marketing communications (B2B) | Legitimate interest, with opt-out |
| Security & fraud prevention | Legitimate interest / Legal obligation |
| Compliance with law | Legal obligation |

### 4. Recipients & Sub-Processors

We share Personal Data with sub-processors listed at [URL]. We update the list with 30-day prior notice.

### 5. International Transfers

We may transfer Personal Data outside its origin region. Where required, transfers rely on adequacy decisions, EU SCCs (2021), UK IDTA, or equivalent mechanisms. Details available on request.

### 6. Retention

We retain Personal Data only as long as necessary for the purposes above and per our Retention Policy. Backup-cycle aging may delay deletion by up to 60 days.

### 7. Your Rights

Subject to applicable law, you may have the rights to: access, rectification, erasure, restriction, portability, objection, and to withdraw consent. To exercise rights, see Part B below or email privacy@[provider].

### 8. Security

We operate an Information Security Management System certified to ISO/IEC 27001:2022, with cloud-specific controls aligned to ISO/IEC 27017:2015 and ISO/IEC 27018:2019. Independent assurance: SOC 2 Type II annually.

### 9. Complaints

You may complain to us at privacy@[provider] or to your supervisory authority (e.g., your national Data Protection Authority, the UAE Data Office, India's Data Protection Board).

### 10. Contact

| Role | Contact |
|------|---------|
| Data Protection Officer | dpo@[provider] |
| Privacy queries | privacy@[provider] |

### 11. Changes

We may update this notice. Material changes will be communicated 30 days in advance via email and/or in-product banner.
