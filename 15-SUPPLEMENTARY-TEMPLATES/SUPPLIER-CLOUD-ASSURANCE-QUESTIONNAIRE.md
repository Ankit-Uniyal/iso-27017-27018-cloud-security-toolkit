# Supplier Cloud Assurance Questionnaire (CSP / Sub-processor)

Document ID: CLOUD-SAQ-001 | Version: 1.0 | Classification: Internal

Use this questionnaire to evaluate prospective and existing CSPs / sub-processors against ISO 27017 and ISO 27018 expectations.

## A. Identity and Certifications

1. Legal entity, registered address, primary jurisdiction.
2. Service in scope (IaaS / PaaS / SaaS) and brief description.
3. Current certifications (ISO 27001, 27017, 27018, SOC 2, FedRAMP, CSA STAR, others). Provide reports under NDA.

## B. Shared Responsibility

4. Provide a Shared Responsibility Matrix for the service in scope.
5. Confirm CLD.6.3.1 alignment.

## C. Technical Security

6. Encryption-in-transit and at-rest standards. BYOK / HYOK options.
7. Tenant segregation approach (CLD.9.5.1) and independent assurance.
8. VM / container hardening baselines (CLD.9.5.2).
9. Identity, MFA, federation, and privileged access controls.
10. Vulnerability and patch management SLAs.
11. Cloud monitoring and tenant-accessible logs (CLD.12.4.5).

12. Are you a PII processor for our data? Confirm role.
13. Documented processing instructions accepted? (A.1)
14. Confirm purpose limitation; no use for marketing / advertising. (A.2)
15. Sub-processor list and notification mechanism. (A.3)
16. Sub-processor flow-down obligations. (A.4)
17. Geographic regions for storage / processing. (A.5)
18. Cross-border transfer mechanism in place (SCCs / BCRs / TIA). (A.15)
19. Breach notification SLA to us (target 24-72 hours). (A.8)
20. Data subject rights cooperation tooling. (A.9)
21. Return / transfer / disposal at termination + deletion certificate. (A.18)
22. Law enforcement disclosure handling. (A.22)
23. Personnel confidentiality and training. (A.19)
24. Independent assurance / audit reports cadence. (A.11)
